About

At Urban Auto Paint, we take pride in being your trusted experts for all your auto body and paint needs. With years of experience in collision repair, custom paint jobs, and vehicle restoration, we are committed to providing exceptional craftsmanship and attention to detail. Our skilled technicians use state-of-the-art equipment and the highest quality materials to restore your vehicle to its original beauty, ensuring a flawless finish every time. Whether you're looking for minor dent repairs or a complete makeover, we’re here to deliver outstanding service, with customer satisfaction as our top priority. At Urban Auto Paint, your vehicle is in the best hands.

Our Services:

  • Collision Repair: We handle everything from minor fender benders to major collision damage, restoring your vehicle to its pre-accident condition.
  • Custom Paint Jobs: Our expert painters create flawless finishes using high-quality paint products, ensuring your car stands out with a perfect coat and color.
  • Dent and Scratch Removal: Say goodbye to unsightly dings and scratches with our precise repair techniques that restore your car’s original look.
  • Frame Straightening: We use advanced equipment to repair your vehicle’s frame after accidents, ensuring structural integrity and safety.
  • Insurance Assistance: We work with all major insurance companies, making the claims process smooth and hassle-free for our customers.
